Pre‑Dive Safety Check

May 3, 2024

Before every dive it’s essential to perform a systematic pre‑dive safety check. Following the BWRAF sequence—BCD, Weights, Releases, Air and Final check—ensures your equipment is secure and functioning correctly. This simple routine helps prevent equipment malfunctions and lets you enter the water confident in your setup and your buddy’s readiness.

In the full article we walk through each element of the BWRAF mnemonic and share tips for incorporating checks into your diving habits. Whether you’re a new student or seasoned diver, a consistent pre‑dive routine enhances safety and enjoyment.
