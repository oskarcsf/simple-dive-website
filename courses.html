Our Courses

Whether you’re taking your first breaths underwater or seeking advanced technical training, Simple Dive offers a comprehensive curriculum taught by certified instructors. Browse our popular programmes below.

Open Water Diver

This entry‑level course introduces you to scuba fundamentals. Through online theory, confined water sessions and open water dives you’ll learn about equipment setup, buoyancy, underwater communication and safety. Upon completion you’ll earn your internationally recognised certification.

Duration:

  • 2 days of pool & theory
  • 2 days of open water diving

Advanced Open Water Diver

Expand your skills and confidence by completing five adventure dives including deep and navigation. You’ll experience different diving activities such as night diving or peak performance buoyancy under the supervision of an instructor.

Prerequisite:

Open Water Diver certification.

Rescue Diver

Learn to identify and prevent problems above and below the surface. This course covers self‑rescue, assisting panicked and unresponsive divers and first aid. It’s challenging yet highly rewarding, boosting your confidence and awareness.

Deep Diver Specialty

Experience the thrill of exploring deeper reefs and wrecks while learning about gas management, nitrogen narcosis and emergency decompression procedures. This specialty expands your depth limit under professional guidance.

Freediver Course

Master the art of breath‑hold diving. You’ll practice breathing and relaxation techniques, equalisation, finning and safety protocols, allowing you to dive deeper and longer on a single breath.
